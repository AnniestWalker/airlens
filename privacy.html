Privacy Policy for AirLens

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

AirLens is an air quality monitoring application that provides real-time air quality information, environmental insights, health recommendations, and pollution alerts.

Information We Collect

Location Information

AirLens requests access to your device location to provide local air quality information, environmental insights, and health recommendations.

Advertising Data

AirLens uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect advertising identifiers and device information according to Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

  • Display air quality data
  • Generate environmental insights
  • Provide AQI alerts
  • Improve application performance
  • Serve advertisements

Data Storage

AirLens stores app settings, AQI history, and alert preferences locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Location Services
  • Air Quality Data Providers
  • Expo Services

Children's Privacy

AirLens is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Your Choices

You may disable location access or notifications through your device settings at any time.
